Our launch standard

Ads can fund play without becoming the game.

This is the proposed placement and reward policy for CrewMultiply Play. Adsterra is the intended display advertising provider, but no Adsterra code is active in the current preview.

AllowedNeverRewardsPrivacyReport an ad

Initial ad formats

Launch with reserved display or native-style placements outside the active game stage: below the game description, between shelf sections, or after a result has been acknowledged. Mobile placements must sit below content rather than floating over it.

CrewMultiply Play will begin with a low frequency, measure exits and return play, and increase nothing automatically. Advertising density is a product-quality metric, not only a revenue setting.

Formats excluded at launch

  • No popunders, clickunders, or surprise new tabs.
  • No notification-like Social Bar units that could be mistaken for game or system messages.
  • No ad over the puzzle board, HUD, menu, pause control, or result.
  • No ad during an active puzzle, construction test, stack, route, or defense wave.
  • No accidental ad button placed where the player was already tapping.
  • No ad that supplies information required to solve a level.
  • No campaign level designed to require a booster or ad continue.

Optional rewards

If rewarded advertising is added later, the exact reward will be shown before playback. Rewards may include a cosmetic reroll, optional visual collection item, or a voluntary second attempt after a completed result. Base campaigns, undo, accessible controls, and fair hints will not be ad-gated.

If an ad fails, the opportunity remains available and the game state is restored. Closing an optional ad cannot remove earned progress.

Advertising privacy

Adsterra scripts will load only after the required privacy choice and a separate activation review. The launch team must document the selected format, data categories, purposes, retention, region controls, audience suitability, and opt-out process before activation. Contextual display advertising is the preferred starting point. See Adsterra’s privacy policy and cookies policy.

Report an ad

Planned reporting contact: ads@crewmultiply.com. The final page will request the date, approximate time, country, game page, screenshot, and any advertiser or destination name without asking for unnecessary personal information.
