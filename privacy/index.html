Who this policy covers

This policy applies to CrewMultiply Play, its browser games, daily challenges, installable web app, and related pages at crewmultiply.com or a designated CrewMultiply Play subdomain. The legal operator will be identified here as [LEGAL ENTITY NAME AND ADDRESS].

Information we plan to handle

Information stored on your device

Game progress, settings, completed levels, streaks, consent choices, and local replay information may be stored in browser storage or an offline cache. This information stays on the device unless a future account or cloud-save feature is clearly introduced.

Basic technical records

Our hosting provider may process an IP address, request time, requested page, browser details, security signals, and error information to deliver and protect the site. Final launch documentation must name the hosting provider and its retention period.

Optional advertising and measurement

Advertising or analytics providers may process device identifiers, IP-derived location, ad interactions, and similar information only after the required privacy choices have been offered. The exact providers, purposes, retention periods, and links to their policies will be listed here before activation.

Adsterra is the intended display advertising provider, but the current local preview does not load Adsterra or analytics scripts. Before activation, the final notice must document the selected format, purposes, data categories, retention, regions, safeguards, and consent or opt-out controls. Provider references: Adsterra privacy policy and Adsterra cookies policy.

Why information is used

  • Provide games, local saves, settings, offline access, and daily challenges.
  • Protect the site against fraud, abuse, malicious traffic, and technical failure.
  • Measure broad performance and improve puzzles when permitted.
  • Display and measure advertising when permitted.
  • Respond to privacy, support, and legal requests.

When information may be shared

Information may be processed by service providers needed for hosting, security, consent management, analytics, and advertising. It may also be disclosed when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as part of a business transaction subject to appropriate safeguards.

CrewMultiply Play does not plan to sell names, email addresses, game saves, or user-created profiles. Whether advertising data constitutes a “sale,” “sharing,” or targeted advertising under a particular law will be disclosed and handled through the required opt-out controls.

Your choices

You can reject optional technologies and continue playing. You can reopen Privacy Choices from every footer. Browser controls can remove local game data and offline caches. Rejecting optional advertising or analytics must not block the base games.

Children

CrewMultiply Play is planned as a general-audience puzzle service for people age 13 and older, not as a service directed to children under 13. We do not plan accounts, chat, user uploads, precise location, or behavioral advertising for known children.

Animal characters alone do not settle whether a service is legally child-directed. Before launch, the complete presentation, audience evidence, marketing, advertising categories, and data collection will be reviewed. If the service is determined to be child-directed or mixed-audience, the data and advertising model must change before collection begins.

Regional privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to know, access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing; withdraw consent; opt out of targeted advertising or data sharing; and appeal a privacy decision. We will not discriminate against you for exercising an applicable right.

A final request method, identity-verification process, authorized-agent process, and response timeline will be added before launch.

Contact
