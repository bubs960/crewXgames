Cookies and local storage

What the site stores, why it is needed, and how optional technologies will be controlled.

Current previewNecessaryOptionalYour choicesLaunch inventory
Pre-launch draft: The final inventory must be generated from the deployed site after the hosting, consent, analytics, and advertising tools are selected.

Current preview

The current preview uses browser local storage for a privacy-choice record and game-specific progress or settings. A service worker may cache game files for faster loading and offline play. Adsterra is the intended display advertising provider, but no Adsterra or analytics script is currently enabled.

Strictly necessary storage

Necessary storage supports requested functions such as saving local progress, remembering accessibility or sound preferences, preserving a privacy choice, securing the service, and providing offline game assets. These functions remain available when optional technologies are rejected.

Optional technologies

Advertising measurement, audience analytics, personalization, and similar technologies will remain disabled until the applicable consent or opt-out requirements have been satisfied. Optional categories must be selectable separately if required in a visitor’s region.

Your choices

Choose “Necessary only” in the privacy panel to keep optional technologies disabled. Choose “Allow optional” only after the final provider list and category controls are implemented. You can reopen the panel below. Browser settings can also clear site data and cached files.

Required launch inventory

  • Storage or cookie name.
  • Provider and domain.
  • Purpose and legal category.
  • First-party or third-party status.
  • Expiry or retention period.
  • Whether it runs before consent.
  • Link to the provider’s policy.
