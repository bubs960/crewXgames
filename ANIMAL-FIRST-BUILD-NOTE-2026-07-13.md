# Animal-First Build Note - 2026-07-13

Direction: build five simple puzzle games, with cats and dogs as the main emotional hook wherever possible. Other animals can support the shelf, but the first library should feel pet-forward rather than random-cozy.

## Current five-game shelf

1. `waddle-home/index.html` - **Knock It Off!**
   - Cat counter mischief.
   - Already existed and solver verification passed earlier today.

2. `mosaic-meadow/index.html` - **Mosaic Meadow**
   - Garden critter tile-rotation puzzle.
   - Already existed and baked-level verification passed earlier today.
   - Future animal pass: add cat/dog cameos only where they do not clutter tile readability.

3. `pup-purr-bento/index.html` - **Pup & Purr Bento**
   - New cat-and-dog block-placement puzzle.
   - The player packs an 8x8 bento tray with cat snacks, dog snacks, and shared treats.
   - Engagement hooks: Daily Bento, best score, lunchbox stickers, combo clears, pet menu packs.

4. `paws-yarn-tangle/index.html` - **Paws & Yarn Tangle**
   - New yarn untangling puzzle.
   - The player drags yarn balls until no yarn lines cross.
   - Engagement hooks: Daily Tangle, before/after share, craft-room upgrades, pet expressions.

5. `pet-parade-sort/index.html` - **Pet Parade Sort**
   - New animal sorting puzzle.
   - The player moves top pets between benches until each row is a matching animal family.
   - Engagement hooks: Daily Parade, best move count, pet costumes, park-photo share card.

## Hub

`games/index.html` links all five games as the **Animal Puzzle Shelf**.
