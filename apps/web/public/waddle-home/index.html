How Counter Cat works
  • Goal: move every orange human object through an arrow-marked edge gap.
  • Controls: swipe anywhere on the counter or use the arrow keys.
  • First cases use Tilt: every movable object slides together. It is the easiest way to learn the room.
  • Blue yarn ♥ is protected: it cannot fall. Use it as a shield, bumper, or gap plug.
  • Beige decoys may fall or stay; dark blocks are too heavy to move. Unlimited undo and free reset mean you can test a route safely.
  • When you want a harder read, switch to Expert. A swipe then moves only the row or column you choose.
  • 📅 Cat Duty is one shared counter a day, and 🏆 Floor is your evidence locker. Shelf sharing stays optional after a case.
